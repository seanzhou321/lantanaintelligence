### 6.5 Bill Splitting and Payment Acknowledgment

The bill splitting system is designed for low-value rental transactions. During the initial release, an automatic rental cost threshold of **$10** determines how each rental is settled:

- Rentals with an agreed cost of **$10 or less** are automatically recorded in the bill splitting system and settled in the monthly cycle.
- Rentals with an agreed cost **above $10** require immediate direct settlement between the Lender and Renter outside the app, consistent with how all payments work on the platform.

This determination is made automatically by the platform. At the end of each month, the app calculates the simplest payment arrangement for Members within a community to settle their bill splitting balances. The app sends notices to debtors, tracks payment acknowledgments from both debtors and creditors, and updates account balances accordingly. All payments are made directly between Members. The app does not process, hold, or transfer funds at any time.

Members acknowledge that where debtors are blocked following unresolved disputes, outstanding credits may be redistributed across active Members in subsequent settlement cycles. See the **Community Bylaws** Article 5.8 for a full description of this risk.
